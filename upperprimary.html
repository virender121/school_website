Primary homeschooling courses

Upper Primary courses

Onlearner Lower Primary homeschooling courses begin with our Getting Ready for Year 1 English and Maths courses. These are typically taken by children aged 4 and 5.

Our Year 1 and 2 courses cover the English National Curriculum and provide a strong foundation for students to move on to Upper Primary. There they will start to develop their skills as independent learners.

The courses are a partnership between you, your child and the Wolsey Hall team. They reflect our philosophy that learning should be enjoyed, and you will work closely with your child and their tutors using easy-to-follow lesson plans.

Our Tutors will support you throughout the courses. Your child will submit assignments regularly and their Tutors will provide detailed and encouraging feedback. You are invited to have a video call with them following each assignment and you will receive progress reports for each subject every three months.

Our Lower Primary homeschooling courses for ages 4-7 provide a strong foundation for children to move up to Upper Primary.

Our Primary Courses

Getting Ready for Year 1 English

Learn More

We believe the teaching & learning of English enables pupils to communicate and understand the world around us. It gives them confidence and a way to express their emotions, opinions and ambitions. We have designed our Junior English Curriculum around the context of a number of texts to immerse and highly engage pupils with English as a subject. The intent is to provide creative, real purpose opportunities through the teaching of SPAG, writing and reading for purpose, which present opportunities for acquiring knowledge, skills and understanding of the English National Curriculum.
